Instructions

Click, hold and drag to pan around the model.
Hold "Ctrl" whilst dragging to change your focus point.
Use the Scroll Wheel/Up and Down Arrows to zoom in and out.
Press "R" to reset your view.

To Trigger Certain Animations:

    Press "D" to open/close the door.
    Press "T" to open/close the dumpster.
    Press "Spacebar" to stop/start the car.
    Press "L" to change whether the point light follows the camera.
    Press "S" to toggle the street lights.
